uni-app官方文档有问题，编译一直报错

import uniPopup from '@/components/uni-popup/uni-popup.vue'

但是,这个引入会报如下的错误

Error: Module build failed (from @/components/uni-popup/uni-popup.vue)
SyntaxError: Indentifier 'uniPopup' has already been declared(47:7)

经仔细查看文档,发现uni-app官方文档和其github上面的文档ß(https://github.com/dcloudio/uni-ui)。
